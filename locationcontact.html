Location

Directions to The Corner

THE CORNER HOTEL IS LOCATED IN THE HEART OF BARCELONA, IN THE EIXAMPLE DISTRICT (LEFT), ON THE CORNER OF MALLORCA STREET AND MUNTANER STREET.

This unbeatable location places the hotel just a few minutes walk from several key places in Barcelona such as Avinguda Diagonal, Plaça Catalunya or Passeig de Gràcia, where you can find elements of the modernist route of the city as important as La Pedrera or Casa Lleó i Morera.

Moreover, the central location of the hotel, in the Eixample district, places it close to the main nightlife, shopping and other places of tourist interest in Barcelona.
