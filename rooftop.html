The Corner Rooftop

Barcelona all around you

Rooftop

The pool at The Corner is located on the hotel’s seventh floor.

This oasis in the heart of Barcelona allows guests to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and relax while taking a refreshing dip in the pool.

Bathers can also enjoy spectacular panoramic views over the city, getting a unique perspective on the neighborhood, the whole of the cityscape, and even Tibidabo.

The Corner Rooftop

The terrace at The Corner Hotel is the ideal place to spend downtime, reading or relaxing, alone or in good company.

Its splendid views, which span from Montjuïc to the Sagrada Familia, allow visitors to disconnect for a bit and get away from the city, without ever having to leave the neighborhood.

And the solarium is the perfect spot to take a sunbath while enjoying Barcelona’s temperate climate.

At sunset, The Corner Rooftop becomes the best spot to enjoy the spectacle of city lights, looking over the city, beneath the glow of the stars.
